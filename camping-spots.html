My Favorite Camping Spots:

Mt. Everest

I love to camp at Mt. Everest to test my ability to withstand extreme tempuratures!

Mt. Fuji

I enjoy camping on Mt. Fuji because sometimes Everest can be a little to cold to my liking.

Any Active Volcano

The Main Reason I camp near live volcanoes is to get warm after my climbs on Mt. Everest and Mt. Fuji.
